The Quoc Story

An avid cyclist and honours graduate of Central St. Martins, Quoc was founded in 2009 by namesake and founder, Quoc Pham, after he found that traditional performance cycling shoes were not best suited to the to and fro nature of city cycling. Quoc's goal was simple: to design a cycling shoe that combined comfort, elegance and style for the daily ride.

The first Quoc urban cycling shoes combined the performance and functionality of the best contemporary bicycle footwear with the style of a casual dress shoe. Notable models included the original Fixed silhouette and the Tourer, both of which became bywords for quality, craftsmanship and performance, helping to establish an urban cycling movement that valued a seamless and stylish transition from pedal to pavement.

Over the years our range has expanded to meet the demands of different cyclists, extending to road cycling shoes and gravel bike shoes, but the Quoc ethos remains the same: to create beautifully made, stylish cycling footwear for both on and off the bike.
